Goodnight, Good Ghost

Created by: Diana Benitez

For those who love halloween and easy reads....

There once was a ghost,
on his way home.
He was a hungry one,
slow and morose.

As part of the Ghost family,
his duty was: go out and join the kids,
to scare them today.

He was one of many creatures
with a big job on Halloween.
He was an image of the holiday
so he had to do his due diligence
to give a bit of freight.

As tired and brain-wired,
as sleepy or weepy,
little good ghost was excited to.
He knew his role,
brought so many smiles. The screams of delight,
from the little bright ones,
they are daring and brave
staring me down.

He flew and went "Wooooooo!"
The ooos and the aaaas sent him into a fit,
of giggles and wiggles all through the night.

Once he was running out of boos, little good ghost went back to the Tombhouse - his family's home. He was happy to see, his friends and family were all filled with glee.
The Vampires, the Felines, the Zombs, and the Weres - were all in the kitchen, cooking to share.
Of all the holidays, this was little good ghosts....

...favorite day.

THE END
